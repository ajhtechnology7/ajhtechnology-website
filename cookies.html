AJH / Cookies

Cookie Policy.

A short, honest read. We don't track you for advertising. We don't sell your behaviour to anyone. Here's exactly what's used and why.

Last updated: 29 May 2026

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small file a website saves to your device to remember something — usually a setting or a session. Cookies are common across the web, but how they're used varies wildly. Some sites use them to build advertising profiles. We don't.

What this site uses.

This is a static website with no tracking, no advertising, and no behavioural analytics. The only cookies and storage that may be set are a handful of strictly necessary technical items from the services that deliver and host the site:

SourcePurposeType
Netlify Hosting and content delivery. May set a session identifier so the right server responds to your request. Strictly necessary
FormSubmit When you submit a contact or audit form, FormSubmit may set a temporary session cookie to validate the submission and prevent spam. Strictly necessary (only when you submit a form)
Google Fonts Serves the Inter and Instrument Serif typefaces. No login required; no behavioural tracking. Strictly necessary

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, advertising cookies, retargeting cookies, or third-party trackers. If we ever add analytics in the future, we'll update this page and ask for your consent first.

Controlling cookies.

You can block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

Because we only use strictly necessary cookies, you don't need to give consent for them under UK law — but you can still block them if you want. The site will work the same way; the contact form may behave slightly differently if you block FormSubmit's session cookie.

Do we use local storage?

No. This site doesn't write anything to your browser's local storage or session storage.

Changes to this policy.

If we add new services or analytics that introduce cookies, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date at the top. The current version always lives at ajhtechnology.digital/cookies.
