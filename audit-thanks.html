Thanks for the detailed brief — that's exactly what we need to do a good job. Here's exactly what happens next, with realistic timings.

01
Quick qualification check Within 24 hours

Harlyn reviews your request personally. We check that AJH is a sensible fit for your business and that we can produce a useful audit. You'll get a short email confirming we're proceeding — or, very occasionally, a polite note explaining why we can't help and who might be better placed.

02
The audit lands Within 48 hours of qualification

A one-page, plain-English PDF in your inbox. Top 3 pages reviewed, mobile/speed flagged, SEO & AEO basics checked, and three prioritised quick wins you can apply this week. No filler.

If you'd like to talk through the audit, we'll book a 20-min call to walk through it. If you'd rather just take the audit and apply it yourself — completely fine, that's the whole point of "free".
