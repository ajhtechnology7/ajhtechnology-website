AJH / About

Built locally. Built to last.

AJH Technology is a small, deliberately-focused agency based in Needham Market, Suffolk. We help local businesses grow with professional websites, sharper brand and smoother processes — all designed to bring you more leads.

Who founded AJH Technology?
AJH Technology was founded by Harlyn Palmer in Needham Market, Suffolk. The company helps small businesses across Ipswich, Stowmarket, Bury St Edmunds and East Anglia grow through three services: lead-generating web design, brand identity, and business process improvement. Every engagement is fixed-price, fixed-scope, and delivered by Harlyn personally.
Harlyn Palmer Founder
From the founder

A letter, basically.

I grew up in Suffolk watching brilliant local businesses struggle with the same problem: their work was excellent, but their digital shop window didn't reflect it. The signwriting was beautiful. The website was tragic. Leads slipped through the cracks. First impressions were costing real money.

AJH Technology exists to fix that. I bring the kind of strategic thinking usually reserved for big companies — and apply it where it matters most: to the bakeries, builders, salons and consultancies that make Suffolk tick.

Outside of work, I'm still chasing a different goal. I trained in the Ipswich Town academy from the age of seven until I was fourteen — seven years that taught me about resilience, hard work, and the value of unglamorous reps. I'm now at Needham Market Football Club, competing to play Step 3 football after graduating from the excellent two-year elite football & education programme inside the Needham Market Academy. The same head-down mentality is what I bring to every client project.

No jargon. No long contracts. Same person on every call. If something doesn't make sense for your business, I'll tell you that instead of selling it to you.

If that sounds like the kind of partner you've been looking for — let's get a coffee.

— Harlyn Palmer
