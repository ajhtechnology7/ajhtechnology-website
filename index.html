The questions we hear most.

Plain answers to the questions Suffolk business owners actually ask us.

How much does a small business website cost in Suffolk?

+
AJH Technology websites start from £1,800 for a focused lead-generation site and £3,500 for a full multi-page build with brand work included. Every project starts with a fixed quote after our free 20-minute strategy call.

Is AI actually worth it for a small business?

+
Yes — when applied to the right tasks. Small businesses using AI for customer enquiries, scheduling and admin typically save 6 to 12 hours per week. Every AI engagement starts with an audit to confirm AI will deliver value. If it won't, we'll tell you honestly.

How long does a project take from start to launch?

+
Most AJH Technology websites go live within three weeks of the kick-off call. Week one is discovery, week two is design and build, week three is launch and tracking setup.

Do you work with businesses outside Suffolk?

+
Yes. We're based in Needham Market and prefer in-person across Ipswich, Stowmarket, Bury St Edmunds, Sudbury, Woodbridge, Felixstowe and Diss, but work remotely with clients across the UK.

What makes you different from other Ipswich agencies?

+
Three things. One: we combine brand, web, process and AI. Two: we're local — same person every call. Three: fixed scope, fixed price, no surprises.

Is my data safe if I adopt AI?

+
Yes. We only deploy AI tools that are GDPR-compliant and built for business use. Every implementation includes a data-handling plan and clear policy guidance for your team.
