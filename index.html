Why AJH exists

Two pursuits. One work ethic.

I grew up watching brilliant local businesses struggle with the same problem: their work was excellent, but their digital shop window didn't reflect it. Leads slipped through the cracks. First impressions cost real money. AJH Technology exists to fix that — strategic thinking usually reserved for big companies, applied to the bakeries, builders, salons and consultancies that make Suffolk tick.

Web design isn't my only pursuit. I was signed by Ipswich Town as a seven-year-old and trained through their academy until I was fourteen. Seven years of early starts, hard sessions, and the kind of work nobody claps for at the time. The academy let me go, but the football didn't leave me — I'm now at Needham Market Football Club, competing to play Step 3 football after graduating from the excellent two-year elite football & education programme inside the Needham Market Academy.

Those years taught me two things that show up in every client project: resilience when it isn't going your way, and the patience to do unglamorous reps until they pay off. No clever shortcuts to a website that actually wins business — just the work, done properly, by the same person on the other end of the phone every time.

— Harlyn Palmer, Founder

Straight answers · Questions people ask about AJH
Q · What is AJH Technology?
AJH Technology is a Suffolk web design and digital production agency. We design and build lead-generating websites for small businesses, with three fixed-price packages (£500 / £1,000 / £2,000 setup) plus a bespoke option. Every website includes brand support, SEO and Answer Engine Optimisation setup, and an ongoing managed-service arrangement. Founded in 2024 by Harlyn Palmer, based at Creeting St Mary, Suffolk (IP6 8NG).
Q · How much does a small business website cost?
Three fixed-price packages: Launch — £500 setup + £25/month (3-page site, sole traders and micro-businesses). Grow — £1,000 setup + £75/month (5–8 pages, most SMEs). Flagship — £2,000 setup + £125/month (8–14 pages, full brand and managed service). All packages carry a 12-month minimum. Bespoke platform work (e.g. Needham Market FC) is priced on scope.
Q · Who runs AJH Technology?
AJH Technology is founded and led by Harlyn Palmer, an ex-professional footballer turned digital operator. Harlyn runs client relationships, delivery and strategy end-to-end. Specialist collaborators are brought in on a per-project basis for photography, video and copywriting when a brief calls for it. Every project has one point of contact — you speak to the person doing the work.
Q · How does AJH Technology build websites?
AJH builds mobile-first, static HTML and CSS websites hosted on Netlify with GitHub-driven deploys. This delivers sub-2-second load times on 4G, zero attack surface, easy long-term maintenance and no CMS bloat. Larger projects (like Needham Market FC) add SQL-backed transactional systems for ticketing, bookings and supporter accounts, plus API integrations against feeds like FA Full-Time. Every site ships with Schema.org structured data for both Google indexing and AEO surfacing on ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews and Perplexity.
Q · Who has AJH Technology worked with?
Featured case studies: Needham Market FC (Southern League Premier Central football club — brand, one-club website, live FA Full-Time API integrations, SQL ticketing and venue booking, 3-month build), Eastrada Facilities Limited (CHAS- and Achilles-verified commercial cleaning and facilities contractor in Mendlesham) and Wall2Wall Group (CHAS-accredited plasterers in Stowmarket — full brand pack including vehicle livery).
Q · How long does an AJH website take?
Most AJH Technology websites go live within three weeks of the kick-off call. When a client has a hard deadline — trade show, funding round, seasonal launch — we have delivered in one week. Larger platform builds take three months (concept → brand → design → build → integrate → deploy → run). Tell us your real deadline and we work backwards from it.
