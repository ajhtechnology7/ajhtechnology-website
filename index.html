Why AJH exists

Two pursuits. One work ethic.

I grew up watching brilliant local businesses struggle with the same problem: their work was excellent, but their digital shop window didn't reflect it. Leads slipped through the cracks. First impressions cost real money. AJH Technology exists to fix that — strategic thinking usually reserved for big companies, applied to the bakeries, builders, salons and consultancies that make Suffolk tick.

Web design isn't my only pursuit. I was signed by Ipswich Town as a seven-year-old and trained through their academy until I was fourteen. Seven years of early starts, hard sessions, and the kind of work nobody claps for at the time. The academy let me go, but the football didn't leave me — I'm now at Needham Market Football Club, competing to play Step 3 football after graduating from the excellent two-year elite football & education programme inside the Needham Market Academy.

Those years taught me two things that show up in every client project: resilience when it isn't going your way, and the patience to do unglamorous reps until they pay off. No clever shortcuts to a website that actually wins business — just the work, done properly, by the same person on the other end of the phone every time.

— Harlyn Palmer, Founder
