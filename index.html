Why AJH exists

Two pursuits. One work ethic.

I grew up watching brilliant local businesses struggle with the same problem: their work was excellent, but their digital shop window didn't reflect it. Leads slipped through the cracks. First impressions cost real money. AJH Technology exists to fix that — strategic thinking usually reserved for big companies, applied to the bakeries, builders, salons and consultancies that make Suffolk tick.

Web design isn't my only pursuit. I was signed by Ipswich Town as a seven-year-old and trained through their academy until I was fourteen. Seven years of early starts, hard sessions, and the kind of work nobody claps for at the time. The academy let me go, but the football didn't leave me — I'm now at Needham Market Football Club, competing to play Step 3 football after graduating from the excellent two-year elite football & education programme inside the Needham Market Academy.

Those years taught me two things that show up in every client project: resilience when it isn't going your way, and the patience to do unglamorous reps until they pay off. No clever shortcuts to a website that actually wins business — just the work, done properly, by the same person on the other end of the phone every time.

— Harlyn Palmer, Founder

The questions we hear most.

Plain answers to the questions Suffolk business owners actually ask us.

How much does a small business website cost in Suffolk?

+
AJH Technology offers three website packages for Suffolk small businesses: Launch (from £500 setup + £25/mo), Grow (from £1,000 setup + £75/mo) and Flagship (from £2,000 setup + £125/mo) — all on a 12-month minimum. For something more custom, our Bespoke option starts with a friendly call. Full details on the pricing page.

How long does a project take from start to launch?

+
Most AJH Technology websites go live within three weeks of the kick-off call. But if you've got a hard deadline — a trade show, a launch event, a season change — we've delivered in one week. Tell us your date and we'll work backwards from there. Your deadline drives the schedule, not ours.

How do you make sure my website actually generates leads?

+
Every site we ship is built around one clear action per page (booking, enquiry or phone call), with analytics and conversion tracking wired up from day one. We tell you which page is doing the work — and which one needs a tweak.

What platform will my website be built on?

+
We pick the right tool for the job. Most lead-generation sites are hand-built for speed and SEO; content-heavy or self-edited sites might use Webflow or WordPress. We'll recommend an option in the strategy call — never lock you into a platform you don't own.

Do you work with businesses outside Suffolk?

+
Yes. We're based in Needham Market and prefer in-person across Ipswich, Stowmarket, Bury St Edmunds, Sudbury, Woodbridge, Felixstowe and Diss, but we work remotely with clients across the UK.

What makes you different from other Ipswich agencies?

+
Three things. One: we design for conversion, not awards. Two: we're local — same person every call. Three: fixed scope, fixed price, no surprises.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

+
Yes. Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support as standard — small tweaks, training, and any teething issues
