What is the AJH AI Workshop?
The AJH AI Workshop is a hands-on, half-day session delivered on-site at your business for £495. It includes a business AI audit, hands-on training with your team, three live AI workflows tailored to your business, an AI policy and data-safety briefing, and 30 days of follow-up support. Up to 8 attendees. Available across Suffolk and East Anglia.

Your morning, hour by hour.

9:00 – 9:30

Audit your business

We walk through your day-to-day with you. Where time is spent, where leads come in, where bottlenecks are. By the end, we know exactly where AI will help — and where it won't.

9:30 – 10:30

Hands-on: the right tools

No slides. We open the tools (ChatGPT, Claude, NotebookLM, Make, etc.) and work through real tasks from your business with your team. They learn by doing.

10:30 – 11:30

Build workflow #1

Live, with your team in the room: we build the highest-value AI workflow for your business. By the end of this hour, it's running.

11:30 – 12:30

Build workflows #2 and #3

Two more live workflows. Customer-service AI, quote drafts, content automation, lead-qualification — whichever fits your business best.

12:30 – 1:00

Policy & safety briefing

A clear, plain-English briefing on AI policy, data handling and GDPR. Your team leaves confident — not anxious — about using AI safely.

Who it's for

Is this right for you?

An honest gut-check before you book.

Yes if
  • You're curious about AI but don't know where to start
  • Your team spends hours on repetitive admin
  • You want practical, not theoretical
  • You'd rather learn by doing than reading
  • You want a Suffolk-based consultant who'll come to you
Not yet if
  • You're already running advanced AI workflows

  • You can't get your team in one room for half a day
