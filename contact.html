Quick answers.

The questions people ask before getting in touch.

Do I need to know what I want before calling?

No. Most clients arrive with "we know something needs to change but we're not sure what". The 20-minute call is designed exactly for that.

How much does it cost to get started?

The strategy call and free website audit are zero-cost. Paid projects start with our Launch package at £500 setup + £25/month (12-month minimum), with Grow and Flagship tiers above that. Standalone Brand and Process projects start from £1,200. Every quote is fixed-price before you commit.

Will you actually tell me if AJH isn't the right fit?

Yes. We've turned down more projects than most agencies have taken on. If you need something we don't do, or if you don't need what we offer, I'll say so — and where I can, I'll suggest who might be a better match.

Do you work outside Suffolk?

Yes — remote across the UK. In-person is reserved for Ipswich, Stowmarket, Bury St Edmunds, Sudbury, Woodbridge, Felixstowe and Diss.
