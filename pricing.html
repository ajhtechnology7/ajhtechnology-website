Two words you'll see on every tier: SEO and AEO.

Plain-English explanations of what they mean and why both matter.

SEO

Search Engine Optimisation

The art of making your site easier for Google to read, understand and rank for the things your customers actually search for. We bake the basics in by default — fast pages, clean structure, the right meta tags, an XML sitemap, and Google Search Console set up so you can see who's finding you and how.

AEO

Answer Engine Optimisation

The new generation of search. ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews and Gemini increasingly answer questions directly — and they pick which sites to quote. AEO uses structured data and clear, factual content so when someone asks an AI "best web designer in Stowmarket?", your business is the one it names.

Pricing questions answered.

The questions Suffolk owners actually ask when comparing packages.

Why is there a 12-month minimum?

+
The monthly fee covers the design, build, hosting set-up, ongoing edits and support across a full year. The 12-month minimum is what lets us price the setup fee as low as it is. After 12 months it moves to a rolling monthly contract — cancel any time with one month's notice.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

+
Yes. Upgrade any time — we'll add the new tier's features and adjust your monthly fee from the next billing cycle. Downgrades are available at the end of your 12-month term.

What's not included? Any hidden fees?

+
No hidden fees — but a few things sit outside every tier and are quoted clearly upfront: hosting (from £8/mo), domain registration & management (from £15/yr), extra change-request hours beyond your monthly allowance (£60/hr), and any third-party tools you choose to use (booking software, email marketing, etc.).

Do I own the website?

+
Yes — the design, the content and the code are yours from day one. We host and maintain it for you, but if you ever leave, you take the site with you.

What counts as a "change request"?

+
Anything you'd like updated, tweaked or added — copy edits, image swaps, new sections, price changes, news posts, opening-hours updates. If it takes longer than your monthly hours allow, we'll quote the extra time before doing the work.

I have an existing website. Can you take it over?

+
In most cases yes — but every existing site is different. We'll do a quick technical audit on the call to confirm. If we can take it over, we'll often suggest the Launch tier as a starting point with a redesign sprint included.

Which package is right for me?

+
Launch suits sole traders and very small businesses who just need a credible online presence. Grow fits established small businesses who want to actively generate leads from the site. Flagship is for ambitious SMEs who want their website to be a serious competitive advantage. Not sure? Book a 20-min call — we'll tell you honestly.
