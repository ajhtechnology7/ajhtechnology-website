What's included in AJH's brand service?
AJH Technology's brand service includes brand strategy, logo design, an identity system (colour palette, typography, voice), brand guidelines as a PDF, business cards and stationery, and Canva-ready social templates. Projects start from £1,200, take two weeks, and are delivered as fixed-price engagements.

How we build your brand.

Three phases. Two weeks. One identity that finally fits.

— PHASE 01 / DAYS 1–3
Discover
We sit down (in person, ideally), and we learn the business: the customers, the personality, the competitors, the moments where your brand has to do the heavy lifting. You'll leave with a strategy document and clear creative direction.
— PHASE 02 / DAYS 4–10
Design
We design two distinct logo directions, then refine the chosen route into a full system: marks, lockups, colour, type, photography style and voice. You see progress daily — no big-reveal surprises.
— PHASE 03 / DAYS 11–14
Deliver
You get the full identity in every format you need: print-ready files, web-ready assets, a brand guidelines PDF, business cards designed and ordered, and Canva templates your team can actually use.
