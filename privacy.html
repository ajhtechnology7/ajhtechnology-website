AJH / Privacy

Privacy Policy.

In plain English: what data we collect, why, how long we keep it, and how to get it back or have it deleted.

Last updated: 29 May 2026

Who we are.

AJH Technology Ltd ("we", "us") is a small web design and digital services agency based at The Office, Grange Farm, All Saints Road, Creeting St Mary, Suffolk, IP6 8NG, United Kingdom. We're the data controller for any personal information you give us through this website. You can contact us on 07488 286207 or via the form on our contact page.

What we collect.

We collect personal information when you fill in a form on this site (for example, the contact form or the free website audit form). Specifically:

  • Your name and business name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (only if you choose to give it)
  • The package or service you're interested in
  • Anything you write in the message field

We don't run any tracking cookies, advertising pixels or behavioural analytics on this site. See our Cookie Policy for the small technical bits that are used.

Why we use it.

We use what you give us to do exactly what you asked: reply to your enquiry, prepare a quote, send you the audit you requested, or arrange a call. We won't add you to a marketing list without asking, and we won't pass your details to any third party for marketing purposes — ever.

Our lawful basis is your consent (you filled in the form to start a conversation) and our legitimate interest in running a business and following up on enquiries.

Who else sees it.

To run the site and reply to you, we use a small number of trusted services that may briefly handle your data on our behalf:

  • Netlify — hosts and serves this website
  • FormSubmit — forwards form submissions to our inbox
  • GitHub — stores the website's source code (no personal data)
  • Google Fonts — serves the typography (no account or login required)

None of these resell your data. They process it strictly to deliver their service.

How long we keep it.

If we don't end up working together, we'll delete your enquiry within 12 months. If we do work together, we keep your contact details for the duration of the project plus six years (for our own records and tax requirements). You can ask us to delete it sooner — see below.

Your rights.

Under UK GDPR, you can ask us to:

  • Send you a copy of the data we hold on you
  • Correct anything that's wrong
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Stop using your data for any specific purpose
  • Hand your data over to another service

To make any of these requests, send a message via the contact form and we'll respond within one calendar month. There's no charge. You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk if you think we've handled your data badly.

International transfers.

Some of the services we use (e.g. Netlify, FormSubmit) may process data on servers in the United States. They operate under UK-recognised data transfer mechanisms (UK-US Data Bridge / Standard Contractual Clauses) so your data stays protected.

Children's data.

This site is intended for business owners over 18. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy.

If we update this policy we'll change the date at the top. The current version always lives at ajhtechnology.digital/privacy.

Get in touch.

Questions about your data? Send a message via the contact form and we'll come back to you personally — same person every call.
