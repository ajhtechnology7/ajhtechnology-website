Audit questions.

The most common questions before requesting an audit.

How much does the audit cost?

Nothing. It's a goodwill gesture for qualifying Suffolk small businesses considering a website project. We do it because it usually starts a useful conversation.

Why do you screen requests?

Audits take real time to produce well. We want to spend that time helping potential clients — not running free competitive intelligence for other agencies. The screening is quick, friendly, and we always reply either way.

Am I obligated to buy anything?

No. The audit is genuinely free with no obligation. If you read it, apply the quick wins yourself and never speak to us again, that's completely fine.

What happens after I submit?

Within 24 hours: we confirm we can help. Within 48 hours of that: your audit lands in your inbox as a PDF. If anything's unclear, Harlyn will email you to ask before producing it.

Don't have a website yet?

No website to audit yet? Skip the form and book a 20-min strategy call instead — we'll talk through what your new site needs to do.
